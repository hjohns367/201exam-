I Love to travel

Traveling is the best. These Japanese cherry blossoms are breathtaking.

  1. Cherry blossoms symbolize life and death
    • When cherry blossoms bloom, we can also shed away unhealthy habits
    • Just like the roses, we can allow ourselves to grow bold and vibrant.
  2. They are a feast for the senses
    • This sensory experience is as rewarding for the eyes as it is the nose
    • There is nothing better than being surrounded by pastel and pink florals!
  3. They help us to appreciate nature
    • These blossoms are also known as 'Sakura', and they are symbolic of Spring. These blooms can teach us a great deal about the nature of life
    • Their short lived existence allows us to appreciate beauty, and they watch it disappear.
